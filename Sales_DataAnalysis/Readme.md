# **Retail Market Performance Dashboard**



* ##### **Project Overview**



The Retail Market Performance Dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of sales performance across multiple dimensions including revenue trends,              product categories, regions, brands, sales channels, and promotional activities.



The dashboard is designed to help stakeholders quickly monitor business performance, identify growth drivers, and make data-driven decisions.



This dashboard analyzes retail sales data from 2022–2024 and presents key business metrics such as total revenue, units sold, and sales growth.

* #### **Dashboard Features**



1\. Monthly Revenue Trend

This visualization shows the monthly trend of revenue and units sold throughout the year.



\- ***Insights***

&#x20; = Revenue steadily increases from January to July.

&#x20; = Peak sales occur in July, followed by a slight decline toward year end.

&#x20; = Units sold closely follow the revenue trend, indicating demand-driven growth.



2\. Top 10 Categories by Revenue

This chart highlights the highest performing product categories based on total revenue.



\- ***Insights***

&#x20; = Yogurt generates the highest revenue among all categories.

&#x20; = Milk and SnackBar categories also contribute significantly.

&#x20; = Juice contributes the least among the top categories.



3\. Revenue by Region

&#x20; = PL-North – 33%

**Channels**

5\. Top 10 Brands by Revenue

\- ***Insights***

### ***Key Business Insights***



**Growth**

* Revenue increased by over 164% between 2022 and 2024, indicating strong business expansion and increasing product demand.



**Category Performance**

* Yogurt is the strongest growth driver, contributing the highest share of revenue.



**Regional Performance**

* PL-South slightly leads in total revenue, while other regions show similar performance.



**Channel Strategy**

* Sales distribution across Discount, E-commerce, and Retail channels is well balanced.



**Promotions**

* Promotional campaigns currently show minimal impact on sales uplift, suggesting the need for better promotional planning.
